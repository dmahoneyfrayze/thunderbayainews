# Blog backlog — LANE 3 daily evergreen queue

The daily weekday engine (CONTENT-ENGINE.md LANE 3) takes the FIRST unchecked `- [ ]` topic,
publishes it, and flips it to `- [x] slug` in the same commit.

**The queue is a ROTATING MIX, on purpose.** The blog was reading as a grant catalog, so the
ordering below interleaves five categories — readers come for useful, opinionated, current-feeling
content; funding pages are the high-intent layer, not the whole diet. Rules:

- Each line starts with its **category label** — use it as the post's `category` field verbatim:
  `Tips` (quick wins, how-tos, plain-language explainers) · `Playbook` (sector guides, "your first
  AI project" pieces) · `Trends` (where things are heading, framed evergreen — the shift and what it
  means for NWO, not this week's headline) · `Perspective` (an argued point of view — the thesis is
  the opinion, but every FACT in it still gets sourced) · `Funding` (program pages).
- **Funding appears at most 1 in every 4 slots.** When refilling, preserve the rotation — never
  stack a category more than twice in a row, and never front-load funding again.
- Evergreen only (no dated news — that's the Signal; news-reactive opinion is the Wednesday op-ed).
- Funding amounts/eligibility verified against `src/data.js` GRANTS_DATA + the official sourceUrl.
  "Confirm eligibility with the program," never "you qualify." No emojis. Direct-answer (or, for
  Perspective, direct-thesis) opening + a source block, every post.
